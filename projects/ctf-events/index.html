How CTF Events works

A quick, focused flow. Steps highlight a live preview and keep you in control.

Start on Home. Upcoming and ongoing events are front and center so you can quickly scan what’s relevant.

Use fast filters and search to narrow down by format, dates, or tags. Results update instantly.

Tap the bookmark to save events you care about. They appear in your dedicated Bookmarks screen.

Set a reminder on any saved event. We’ll notify you ahead of time so you never miss a start.

Add key events to your device calendar, with times and links included.

FAQs

Is CTF Events free?
Yes. It’s free with minimal ads. You can purchase lifetime premium (one‑time) to remove ads.
What features are included?
Search, filters, bookmarks, notifications/reminders, links to registration pages, and calendar integration.
Does it work offline?
You can browse cached results when offline. For the latest events and prices, you’ll need an internet connection.
