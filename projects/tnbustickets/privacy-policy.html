Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TN Bus Tickets. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Bus Ticket Resale application ("App"). By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

2.1 Account Information (Linked to Your Identity):

  • User ID: A unique identifier generated by Firebase Authentication
  • Device ID: A unique identifier generated from your device information (Android ID or iOS Identifier for Vendor)
  • Email Address: Collected only if you sign in with Google (optional)
  • Display Name: Collected only if you sign in with Google (optional)
  • Account Type: Whether you are using an anonymous or Google account
  • Account Creation Date: Timestamp of when your account was created

2.2 Ticket Information (Linked to Your Identity):

  • Source and Destination: Cities for bus routes
  • Departure Date and Time: When the bus departs
  • Price: Ticket price in Tunisian Dinars (TND)
  • Car Number: Vehicle identification number
  • Phone Number: Your contact phone number (shared with potential buyers)
  • Description: Optional additional details about the ticket
  • Ticket Status: Available, sold, or cancelled
  • Buyer/Seller IDs: User IDs of parties involved in transactions

2.3 Device Information (Not Linked to Your Identity):

  • Device Type: Android or iOS
  • Operating System: OS version information
  • Device Identifiers: Used to generate a unique device ID stored locally

2.4 Authentication Data:

  • Google Account Information: If you choose to sign in with Google, we access your email and profile name through Google Sign-In
  • Firebase Authentication Tokens: Used to maintain your session

Note: We do NOT collect:

  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Photos or images (image upload functionality is not currently active)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Provision:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To enable you to list tickets for sale
  • To enable you to search and purchase tickets
  • To facilitate communication between buyers and sellers (by sharing phone numbers)
  • To track ticket status (available, sold, cancelled)

3.2 Authentication and Security:

  • To authenticate your identity
  • To prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • To maintain account security

3.3 Support and Communication:

  • To respond to your support requests
  • To send you important service-related notices

3.4 Legal Compliance:

  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • To respond to legal requests

We do NOT use your information for:

  • Advertising or marketing purposes
  • Tracking you across other apps or websites
  • Selling your data to third parties
  • Analytics or usage tracking

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 With Other Users:

  • When you list a ticket: Your phone number and ticket details are visible to potential buyers
  • When you purchase a ticket: Your user ID is shared with the seller to facilitate the transaction
  • This sharing is necessary for the core functionality of the App

4.2 With Service Providers:

  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Storage. Your data is stored on Google's servers. Google processes this data according to their Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Sign-In: When you use Google Sign-In, Google processes your authentication according to their Privacy Policy
  • These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify

4.3 Legal Requirements:

  • We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • We may disclose information to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users

4.4 Business Transfers:

  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity

We do NOT share your information with:

  • Third-party advertisers
  • Data brokers
  • Analytics companies
  • Social media platforms

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage Location:

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers, which may be located outside your country of residence. By using our App, you consent to this transfer.

5.2 Security Measures:

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication through Firebase
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular security updates

5.3 Data Security Limitations:

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

5.4 Local Storage:

Your device ID is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms (SharedPreferences on Android, UserDefaults on iOS).

6. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

6.1 Firebase Services (Google):

  • Firebase Authentication: Handles user authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores your account and ticket data
  • Firebase Storage: Available for future image storage (currently not in use)
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6.2 Google Sign-In:

  • Provides authentication services when you choose to sign in with Google
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6.3 Device Info Plus:

  • Used to generate a unique device identifier (stored locally only)
  • Privacy Policy: https://plus.fluttercommunity.dev/privacy

6.4 URL Launcher:

  • Used to open email client for support requests
  • Does not collect or transmit data

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand their data practices.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Correction:

  • You can view your account information in the Profile screen
  • You can update your display name and email (if using Google Sign-In) through your Google account settings

7.2 Account Deletion:

  • You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the App's Profile screen
  • When you delete your account, we will delete your user data, all tickets you have listed, and all reviews you have created
  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Note: Some information may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes (e.g., completed transactions)

7.3 Data Portability:

  • You can request a copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format
  • Contact us at the email address below to make this request

7.4 Opt-Out Options:

  • You can use the App anonymously without providing email or name
  • You can sign out of your Google account at any time
  • You can stop using the App at any time

7.5 Exercising Your Rights:

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: contact@abidiahmed.com

We will respond to your request within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

8.1 Active Accounts:

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active and you are using our services.

8.2 Deleted Accounts:

If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except:

  • Information we are required to retain by law
  • Information necessary for legitimate business purposes (e.g., completed transaction records)
  • Anonymized data that cannot be linked back to you

8.3 Ticket Data:

  • Active ticket listings are retained until the ticket is sold, cancelled, or expired
  • Historical ticket data may be retained for up to 2 years for transaction records
  • After this period, ticket data is anonymized or deleted

8.4 Device ID:

  • Device IDs are stored locally on your device and are not automatically deleted
  • You can clear app data to remove the device ID (this will require creating a new account)
